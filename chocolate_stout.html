Chocolate Stout

This chocolate stout is like liquid chocolate!

This beer is for chocolate lovers. It has a distinct bitter dark chocolate flavor, full body, and amazing chocolate aroma. The addition of the lactose is a must, without it the body will be lacking and the beer will be too bitter to enjoy.

Batch Size: 5 Gallons 7.5 lbs Maris Otter
Original Gravity: 1.053 1 lbs Caramel/Crystal malt 80L
Final Gravity: 1.013 1 lbs Chocolate Malt
SRM: 39 8 oz Roasted Barley
IBU's: 24.7 2.00 oz Crystal hops (3.10%AA) 90 minutes
Total Grain: 9.5 lbs 0.50 oz Crystal hops (3.10%AA) 30 minutes
WLP004 Nottingham Ale Yeast 8.00 oz Cocoa Powder
12 oz Milk Sugar (Lactose) Chocolate extract (optional)
  1. Strike with 180 degree water for 150 degree mash, 60 minutes
  2. Sparge 8 gallons
  3. Boil hops for 90 minutes
  4. Chill, measure gravity, pitch yeast
  5. Ferment in primary for 14 days at 64 degrees
  6. Ferment in secondary for 14 days at 64 degrees
  7. At bottling, measure gravity again, boil 1.50 cups of water and 3.5 oz corn sugar, add sugar water to bottling tun
